This is a demonstration of AI based Detection of Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) Using X-ray Images and Deep Convolutional Neural Networks.The neural network contains pre-trained transfer models and Chest X-ray images. For this purpose, we have used VGG16 pre-trained models and on top of it, neural network was built with X-ray image dataset shared by Mendeley & Qatar University in Kaggle.
